Little Stars Camp

Ages 3 - 5 Years

What do you get when you combine the magic of Broadway with the fun and freedom of Summer? The ultimate Summer Camp experience for your Preschooler!

Each week, your Little Star will be introduced to the world of a new Broadway Musical as Stages’ Camp Counselors bring the show to life through music, dance, dramatic play, and process art activities. Snack time, story time, sensory play, outdoor adventures to Mary Bartleme Park (weather permitting), and of course, Bubble Intermission, round out the daily routine

Having trouble separating? That’s okay! Our Camp Counselors will work with you and your Little Star to develop an individualized separation plan that will ease the transition and have them saying “So Long, Farewell” in no time!
