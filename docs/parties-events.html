Parties & Events

Let Stages Performing Arts provide our special brand of birthday magic!

Stages’ team of professional performers will bring your child’s favorite characters to life in an interactive show that will have them singing, dancing, and playing along! From entertainment at your home or event space to a fully-catered party at one of our partner venues, Stages has a variety of packages to meet your party needs!

Choose from classic Broadway shows and popular themes or work with us to create a party that’s perfect for your child’s special day!

Choose from one of the following packages or let us know your specific needs and we’ll create a custom package for your party!
